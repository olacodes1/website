About Me

I'm Olanrewaju Olaniyi (a.k.a Olacodes), a digital designer at The Sole Company, Nigeria. I help businesses create usable, accessible, functional and beautiful digital products. I am passionate about solving complex problems through data driven processes that translate business objectives into solutions, putting empathy for the users as a key ingredient.

My objective is to gather more experience and knowledge in niche, and to secure a career opportunity that expands my knowledge and fully utilize my knowledge, practice and skills, while ensuring result-oriented efforts in boosting the productivity of organizations.

Except working with other designers who handle UX processes, most of my personal projects are based on concept, but I try to bring accessibility and usability into the equation to improve the experience.

Animes, movies, football, and games are my compainion whenever I'm free or going through stress. They hep me a lot. My explorations involve Coding, Design System, Interaction Design, and the Word of God.
