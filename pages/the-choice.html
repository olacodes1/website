About The Choice?

Just like the other food ordering apps, The Choice gives users a better experience, and make finding their cravings better by scanning food they see to see if it's availabe on the app. And it offers variety of food based on your location/tribe, i.e local foods, and many more.

The Problem

I checked most of the reviews on food delivery apps and people keep complaining on how hard it is to navigate through the app. And I get to talked to some people and they went into more detail about the proble they faced using theor favorite food delivery apps.

Solution

To give the users a better experience and ease of access so that the users can quickly get job done with a little time.
